Program

  • Keynote Speaker： Lei Cui

    Title: Document AI: Benchmarks, Models and Applications

    Bio: Dr. Lei Cui is a Principal Researcher in Natural Language Computing (NLC) group at Microsoft Research Asia. Lei joined MSRA in 2015 after he received his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at Harbin Institute of Technology. His research interest is Natural Language Processing, including machine translation, information extraction, text generation, question answering, dialog system, etc. His work has contributed to many important products in Microsoft such as Azure, Office, and Bing. He published more than 30 research papers in top conferences of AI and NLP areas, such as ACL, AAAI, IJCAI, KDD, EMNLP, COLING, etc. Besides, he also served as the reviewer for ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, COLING, and many other conferences. Besides, he also held 15 U.S. patents in AI and NLP. During his Ph.D. study, he won the 2011 Microsoft Fellowship Award and the 2014 IBM Fellowship Award.

  • Keynote Speaker： Lianwen Jin

    Title: Visual Information Extraction for Document Understanding

    Bio: Lianwen Jin received the B.S. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China, and the Ph.D. degree from the South China University of Technology, China, in 1991 and 1996, respectively. He is currently a Professor at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology. He is the author of more than 200 scientific papers. He has served as AC/SPC/PC member for many international conferences, including ICDAR, ICFHR, ICPR, CVPR, ICCV, IJCAI, AAAI, etc. Dr. Jin was a recipient of the award of New Century Excellent Talent Program of MOE in 2006 and the Guangdong Pearl River Distinguished Professor Award in 2011. His research interests include optical character recognition, handwriting analysis and recognition, document understanding, deep learning and computer vision.
