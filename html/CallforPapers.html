Submission:

The first WDIL is calling for original and unpublished papers on topics of interest as above . The submitted papers should be no more than 6 pages. Since this year, the ICDAR main conference planned to publish the proceedings with Springer LNCS. Papers should be formatted according to LNCS instructions for authors. Papers have to be submitted via the workshop's Easychair submission page.
