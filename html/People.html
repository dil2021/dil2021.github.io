  • Cheng-Lin Liu

    Institute of Automation of Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), China E-mail: liucl@nlpr.ia.ac.cn

    Cheng-Lin Liu is a Professor at the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, and is now the director of the laboratory. He the Ph.D. degree in pattern recognition and intelligent control from the Chinese Academy of Sciences in 1995. During 1996 to 2004, he was a postdoctoral fellow and later a researcher in Korea and Japan.His research interests include pattern recognition, image processing, neural networks, machine learning, and especially the applications to character recognition and document analysis. He won the IAPR/ICDAR Young Investigator Award of 2005. He is an associate editor-in-chief of Pattern Recognition Journal and an associate editor of several journals. He is a Fellow of the CAAI, the IAPR and the IEEE.

  • David Doermann

    University of Buffalo, E-mail: doermann@buffalo.edu

    David Doermann is currently a Professor with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University at Buffalo and the Inaugural Director of the University at Buffalo Artificial Intelligence Institute.   • Errui Ding

  • Hua Wu

    Baidu Inc., China, E-Mail: wu_hua@baidu.com

    Hua Wu is the chief scientist of Baidu NLP and the chair of Baidu technicalcommittee. Her primary research areas are machine translation and dialogue systems, and she has also worked on question answering, knowledge graph, and distributed representation.   • Jingtuo Liu

    Baidu Inc., China, E-Mail: liujingtuo@baidu.com
